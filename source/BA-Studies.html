Business Development Plan

The assignment was to structure a scattered project repository with 160+ initiatives enabling efficient planning, prioritization and resource management.

Together with team members from Customer Fulfilment and Digital we created a structured process for how new initiatives are to be handled, maintained and closed. In my role as Business Development Analyst, I introduced these processes in workshop/teams session and 1:1 talks. Leading workshops, creating effective Powerpoint presentations, organizing initiatives in Microsoft Planner as our main tool, lead to an effectful structure and communication within the organisation. By creating weekly status reports based on parameters like RAG Level, progress and priority the initiatives being part of the plan. The plan inlcuded consideration of business strategies, KPIs and objectives to achieve long-term growth within Customer fulfilment. Reports I created based on data from Excel for utilization in monthly management meetings and recurring global team meetings to show the progress of the different projects.

Sales Information Move-Out

MHS (Retail System) is a core application used in 360+ stores worldwide. Initially focused on Range and Supply, the system expanded over time to serve various demand areas in Retail stores. However, Sales Information is no longer considered essential within the core functionality as there are better systems available for this purpose. The project involved replacing and relocating sales-related functions from MHS to systems such as Return & Recovery and Payment.

Deliverables

NOTE: Rewrite deliverables. I analyzed and documented sales-related functions in the MHS system, identified suitable systems for these functions, and translated them into workflows in systems like Return & Recovery and Payments. The workflows were adapted to comply with rules and guidelines, serving as a basis for transformation. The work contributed to Change Management & Communication across 40+ countries and 400+ stores worldwide. As I joined the program late, my main focus was on analyzing the "Sales Adjustments" screen removal and new stock structure in MHS.

Technology

Retail System MHS, Payment System, Return & Recovery, Reporting, Clearquest, Clearcase, Cognos

Role

Senior Business Analyst (April - December 2018)

Office 365 Migration

The customer, a company providing strategic solutions in drug development, had the need of consolidating their office 365 platform by reducing licences and integrating a collaboration platform. Since there are a couple of collaboration tools available on the market it was required to compare the most common ones (Sharepoint Online, Box, Dropbox). Data should be consolidated and moved to one common platform. The analyse provided input on the way forward implementing a suitable collaboration tool usable for both customer communication and internal use. As a result the decision was made to use Sharepoint Online.

Analyze and present a data integration and office 356 consolidation model to efficiently share documents between the customer internally and their respective customers. Analyse and explain Office 365 licensing models to consolidate the current Office 356 subscriptions to one subscription suitable for the company with 10 users. Prepare for consolidation of two office 356 tenants to one commonly used tenant.

Deliverables

Analyse most common of-the-shelf solutions available for collaboration Evaluation and presentation of different possible file sharing combinations and packages Evaluation of license costs and benefits Analyze best solutions for consolidation of tenants

Technology

Office 365, Sharepoint Online, Dropbox for Business, Box for Business

Role

Business Analyst (October - December 2017)
